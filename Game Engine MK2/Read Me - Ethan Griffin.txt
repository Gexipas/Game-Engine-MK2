Controls

F - Toggle between terrain walk and free flying
G - Toggle Grass visability
R - Toggle Wireframe
E - Toggle between fountain, snow and none

Terrain walk
mouse - rotate camera
W - move forward
S - move backward
A - move left
D - move right

Free flying
mouse - rotate camera
W - move forward
S - move backward
A - move left
D - move right
Spacebar - move up
Left Control - move down
Shift - Increase fly speed by x5
